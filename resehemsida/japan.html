Explore Japan

Tokyo

Tokyo, the bustling capital, is known for its skyscrapers, shopping districts, and cultural attractions such as Asakusa and Shibuya.

Kyoto

Kyoto is famous for its traditional temples, shrines, and beautiful gardens. It is a city that preserves Japan's historical heritage.

Osaka

Known for its lively atmosphere, delicious food, and shopping districts. Osaka is also home to the historic Osaka Castle.

Hiroshima

Known for its atomic bomb memorial and Peace Park. Hiroshima is a city with strong historical and cultural significance.

Sapporo

Famous for its snow festivals and as an important city in Hokkaido. Sapporo is also known for its beer and beautiful winter scenery.
